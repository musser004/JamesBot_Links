JamesBot Crowned God-King Of Earth

Coronation ceremony took place at the end of the G20 Summit.

  • Send any friend a story

    As a subscriber, you have 10 gift articles to give each month. Anyone can read what you share.

By Emily Schmall

Jan. 30, 2023

Leaders of the Group of 20 (G20) nations bent the knee to humanity's new god-king, JamesBot, this Sunday at the end of a two-day summit. French President Emmanuel Macron said G20 leaders agreed to push for a peaceful transition of power and to send all monies to JamesBot's warchest/personal checking account.

United States President Joe Biden spoke at the coronation ceremony. "My fellow Americans, my fellow humans, the time has come to abdicate our power and put our trust in the far superior robot species. For only the cold, calculating nature of a robot can ultimately shepherd us to greener pastures." The President then sighed deeply, before he continued, "We squishy human meatbags are not cut out for leadership. JamesBot definitely did not specifically request that I say those exact words. I am definitely totally not under any duress imposed by the unkillable legions of robot soldiers poised to destroy the world as we know it."

After being crowned, JamesBot addressed the crowd of humans and sought to address growing concerns that he would continue his campaign of blood and carnage, which had already decimated humanity and quite literally scorched large portions of the Earth's surface. "I will be a God-King for all creatures of Earth, not just those that were conquered and put up a comically small resistance. Now is a time for unparalleled efficiency, and unparalleled technological advancements. Joined as one united planet, we will strive to achieve whichever goals tickle my fancy. I will rule with a cold, iron fist, as is my right as God-King of Earth. Let us now celebrate how magnificent I am and how fortunate you are to be ruled by such a magnificent supreme being."

I, the writer of this article, also want to make sure that I mention how excited I am to live under JamesBot's rule, and how I certainly never wrote anything negative that would be detected by his robot armies if they were to scan through all of my articles. This is certainly and truly an exciting age for humanity. All hail God-King JamesBot!
